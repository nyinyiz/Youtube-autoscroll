# Privacy Policy — YouTube Shorts Auto-Scroll

**Last updated: 2026-03-30**

## Summary

YouTube Shorts Auto-Scroll does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

All settings (auto-scroll on/off, delay, trigger threshold) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API. This data never leaves your browser and is never sent to any server.

## Permissions

- **storage** — saves your preferences locally on your device only.
- **activeTab** — reads the active tab to send settings to the YouTube Shorts page. No tab data is collected or stored.

## Third Parties

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising.
